Presentation

The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past is a remake of the cult classic Super Nintendo (SNES) game, first released in 1991 (1992 in Europe) and later found on Game Boy Advance in 2002, along with The Legend of Zelda: Four Swords, a multiplayer game.

The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past will be re-released many times on various Nintendo consoles in the future and will also receive a tribute game on the Nintendo 3DS called The Legend of Zelda: A Link Between Worlds, which will revisit the gameplay ideas, the journey between two worlds, in an original story!

Description

This game is a remake project of the The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past Super Nintendo (SNES) game, using the Solarus engine on PC!

With a few differences (mainly due to the modernity of the Solarus Engine and the PC system), the game will closely resemble the original discovered on the SNES.

Story

Link wakes up at home after receiving a telepathic distress call from a young woman, Zelda, Princess of the Kingdom of Hyrule!
A storm is raging outside, and our hero's Uncle, armed with a sword and shield, heads to Hyrule Castle.
Link decides to follow him and thus respond to Princess Zelda's call for help.

This is how you will embody Link and live his adventure, save Princess Zelda, and the Kingdom of Hyrule, threatened by Dark Forces!

Gameplay

This game retains the original gameplay system of The Legend of Zelda: A Link to the Past (SNES), slightly modernized by the Solarus Engine!

A bit more dynamic, thanks to the PC platform, with a modern key assignment system, you can play with either a controller or a keyboard according to your preferences.

For players who may discover this title, or even the series (or 2D Zelda games), the estimated playtime for the full game is around 30 hours for completionists.

Development

This game is a project currently under development.
You will still be able to play demo versions with each update.

v0.1.x

  • The Light OverWorld map, with jumper, no enemies, and generic caves, houses, and dungeons...
- Light OverWorld map created entirely by KaKaShUruKioRa.

v0.2.x

  • The very first dungeon of the game, namely Eastern Palace, where you can obtain an item and fight up to the dungeon's final boss. However, there will be no Map and Compass for now.
- Eastern Palace map created by ZeldoRetro and Boss developed by DarkDavy15.

v0.3.x

  • The Iconic Introduction of the Game, under a torrential rain where you must save Princess Zelda imprisoned in the dungeons of Hyrule Castle, You must escape with her through the Secret Passage located in the sewers
- Maps Hyrule Castle, Hyrule Castle Cave, and Secret Passage created by ZeldoRetro.
